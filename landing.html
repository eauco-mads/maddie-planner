✦ Questions

Good things to know

Do I need to know my birth time? ↓
Your birth time determines your Rising sign, which is the foundation of your entire house system — it's the most important piece of data in the chart. If you know it exactly, great. Approximate (within an hour) still works well. If you don't have it, we'll note it and work with what we have — but the planner will be less precise, particularly for Rising sign and house placements.
What if I don't know my Human Design type? ↓
That's completely fine — it's optional on the intake form. If you leave it blank, we'll focus on your Vedic chart and you can share your HD info later if you find it. You can get your Human Design chart free at any HD chart calculator (myhumandesign.com is a good one).
How long does it take to receive my planner? ↓
5–7 days from when you submit your intake form. We pull your full chart, write your month-by-month content, and build your interactive planner from scratch — it takes real time because it's actually personalized.
What's the difference between this and a birth chart reading? ↓
A reading gives you a snapshot — a conversation about your chart on one day. This is a 14-month living tool you use every day. It's built from your chart, but it's designed for daily navigation: the moon phases, the transits, the planetary day energy, the prompts — all of it mapped to your specific houses and placements so you know what to do with the information every morning.
Can I cancel anytime? ↓
Yes — monthly subscription, cancel anytime through Stripe. Your planner link stays active for the duration of the period you've paid for. No contracts, no lock-ins.
What does my planner actually look like? ↓
It's a beautiful interactive HTML page — a monthly calendar you can click through, a Today card that shows your daily energy, a full glossary of astrology and HD terms, moon phase tracking, a mood log, and journal prompts. All saved privately in your own browser. Try the free version to see exactly what the experience is like.
